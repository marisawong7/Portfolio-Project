About Me.

Hello! I'm Marisa, a software engineer based in NYC. I'm a sophomore at the University of Michigan's College of Engineering studying Computer Science. I'm interested in technology that can make people's everyday lives easier; anything from a device as essential as our phones, to a simple weather widget that saves the time of opening the app or turning on the TV. I'm currently involved in organizations such as BECO, GEECS, SWE, and VOID Tech Consulting. In my free time, I like to go for a run, play soccer, play pool, try new restaurants and hang out with friends.
